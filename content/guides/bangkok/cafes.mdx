## How we rate cafes

* **WiFi**: tested with fast.com on a Tuesday morning.
* **Plugs**: how many seats have a power socket within reach.
* **Noise**: at peak hours (\~11am).
* **Calls**: whether you can actually take a Zoom call without people staring.
* **Best time to go**: when seats are easy + WiFi isn't loaded.

## Use cafes like this

<ProTip>
  **Three-cafe rule.** Pick 3 cafes within walking distance of your condo and rotate between them. One quiet (deep work), one social (lighter tasks + lunch), one with plugs (longer sessions). You'll get through 80% of your work weeks on those three.
</ProTip>

## When a cafe is *not* enough

* You take 3+ Zoom calls a day.
* You need to be at a desk for 8+ hours.
* You hate having to buy a £4 coffee every hour to keep your seat.

In that case, you want [coworking](/guides/bangkok/app/coworking).
